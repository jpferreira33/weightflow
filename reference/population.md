# Example target population for weightflow

A simulated population (sampling frame) of individuals nested in
households, with demographic auxiliaries and two outcomes. Generated by
`data-raw/weightflow_data.R`.

## Usage

``` r
population
```

## Format

A data frame with one row per person and the columns:

- person_id:

  individual identifier

- household_id:

  household identifier (cluster)

- region:

  region: North, South, East or West

- sex:

  F or M

- age:

  age in years (18-95)

- income:

  annual income

- employed:

  employment indicator (0/1)
